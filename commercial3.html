PANIF BAKERY

The owners of a small bakery on the countryside decided to renovate it in order to offer it a fresher and more inviting look.

The inspiration for this design was taken from the french culture for baking bread. The first thing you see when you enter is the fresh bread on the shelves. Its freshness is increased by the pastel colors of the interior and the well organized space.

Type: Hair and Nail Salon
Location: Bucharest, Romania
Area: 215 sq.m
