ABOUT ME

My journey as an interior architect started in 2013, after 5 years of studies and a strong desire to finally be able to implement everything I learned and to learn much more on the way.

Since then I have worked with clients from several countries, helping with their homes and their businesses. I learned to addapt to different cultures and always put the clients needs first while continuing to be creative.

I am confortable with offering a complete set of services, including the creative, the technical and the implementation part. I am continuously improving and always following my core values.

2015-2017 Interior Architect for 2Deco Studio, Bucharest Romania

Education
2008-2013 Bachelor and Master Degree in Interior Architecture, "Ion Mincu" University, Bucharest
